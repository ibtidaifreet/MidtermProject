How are we forming missed connections?

What about myself do I not know?

What about us do we know?

We know a lot yet almost nothing.

Unless, we try to.

Most of are broken people making broken connections. And somehow we never realise that until it is too late.

Somehow, it is when we feel unheard by the people around us do we feel the most heard.

What are we forgetting in the midst of forming connections?

Most of the closest relationships we form are based out of our early childhood experiences

Some of us maybe had a parent who was never there for us.

Some of us had a parent who never knew how to comfort us when we needed them for our support. What did that result to?

We are somehow drawn to the pain that feels familiar and are stuck in...

insecure or avoidant attachments.

A lot of us, actually a whopping 40% of us are stuck in a relationship where we hurt more than we love being stuck in such attachments.

So how do we break ourselves from the shackles of broken connections?

It starts with you. It starts with how you take charge of your own narrative and reflect.
